Kristian Watson-Noonans



1945

The first atomic weapon is dropped on Japan, leading to catastrophic casualties, it was the dawn of the nuclear age, and, the beginning of the Cold War.

Japan surrenders to the Allied Powers, marking the end of World War II. At this point, Fallout’s timeline and our own, split apart.

1960

The U.S shrinks politically from 50 states to 13.

2002

West Tek is founded

Destined to become the single largest private contractor to the U.S. government, its focus is on advanced weaponry and biomedical research.

2025

Robert house is born

Founder of Robco industries, he is the reason technology is different from our own. It looks very much like the ‘50s and 60’s, TVs and radios seem frozen in time and the technology remains popular for many decades. Meanwhile, atomic power is put to use for the public, Robert House helped give birth to many of the universe’s robots including Mr. Handy and even the Pip-Boy.

2052

The Resource Wars begin

All of the oil begins to dry out worldwide, in desperation, Europe invades the middle east.

2052

The U.N is disbanded in spite of the chaos.

2053

An atomic weapon destroys Tel Aviv, Israel, leading to a nuclear exchange between middle eastern leaders.

2054

Vault tec builds the first vaults in fear of nuclear annihilation, But, they were used for secret experiments.

2060

With the oil completely dry, all vehicles stop and only fusion powered cars are available, The European commonwealth soon collapses.

2066

China invades Alaska for the last oil reserves, The first battle starts in Anchorage.

2072

America invades Canada to make a shortcut to Alaska.

2073

The enclave is formed to ensure the continuity of government in case of nuclear war, They take the president to a private oil rig in the pacific ocean and vow to eliminate all radioactive species and repopulate the U.S with humans.

2077

Alaska is retaken from China.

Over a period of two hours the world burns, While no one knows who strikes first, nearly every president and prime minister launches their warheads. The land itself becomes plastic from impacts so powerful, they cause the Earth’s fault lines to shift, creating mountain ranges and oceans where there were none before.

2287

Two centuries after the bombs fell, the sole survivor emerges from vault 111 and your story in the boston commonwealth begins.
